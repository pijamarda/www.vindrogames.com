A Greek Tale

Complete each level by hacking the computers at the end of the circuits.

You must take care that your Trojan does not hit the Firewalls! Your Trojan horses will be sent automatically from the beginning of each level. To avoid the Firewalls, you must use buttons w & s to jump from one circuit to another. You will complete a level when all three computers are infected.
